Welcome to the Electronics Physics Hub!

This website explores the foundational physics driving the most widely used electrical components. Whether you are an engineer or a curious learner, exploring how charges and fields behave at a fundamental level will significantly enchance your circuit design abilities.

Explore Our Core Learning Modules

Please browse through our specific subpages to study how different physics concepts drive different types of components:

  • Passive Components: An overview of elements that store, dissipate, or manipulate energy within electric or magnetic fields without external power.
  • Active Components: A summary solid-state active hardware that controls current flow thorugh localized gating barriers and semiconductor structures.
  • Integrated Circuits: A explanation of various circuits where billions of miniature components are condensed into individual microchips.
